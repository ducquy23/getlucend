About Lucend

Driven by curiosity, we operate with a purpose-driven mindset, always focused on the bigger picture. We foster collaboration, nurture creativity, and prioritize collective efforts over individual ambitions.

Our mission is a future

where all data center organizations in the world are transformed into intelligent ecosystems, becoming responsible and conscious users of resources, driving a sustainable digital infrastructure.

Collaboration, innovation, and shared moments

Vision

The world needs data, but the planet needs energy. Data centers consume colossal resources, and we are here to change that equation. Lucend is a team of engineers, data scientists, and visionaries building a unique AI-driven platform.

We transform complex data center operations into intelligent ecosystems, saving up to 40% in energy and water. Our mission is to reduce global energy
consumption for data center cooling by 1%.

More than just a job in Tech

We are building the kind of company we want to work for.
Our culture stands on four pillars:

Creativity

We give ourselves permission to experiment, try bold ideas, and learn without fear of failure.

Drive

We’re personally committed to making a real difference in sustainability, and we show up with energy and purpose every day.

Openness

We genuinely listen to each other, welcome diverse perspectives, and grow stronger through honest collaboration.

Gezelligheid

We create a warm, playful, and human workplace where joy, trust, and empathy are just as important as results.
