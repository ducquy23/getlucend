Introducing Lucend — A letter from the founders of Coolgradient

Jasper de Vries

René Gompel

Why we're evolving
‍

Over the past several years, Coolgradient has proven that human-in-control, Transparent AI can drive extraordinary gains in energy efficiency and uptime. Our customers across the globe have cut PUE by up to 40%, extended infrastructure life, and built new trust in optimization that shows its work.

‍

But the name Coolgradient carries limits that simply no longer serve where we're heading. What once spoke to cooling optimization now anchors perception there. Yes, we optimize cooling. But our platform's capabilities reach far beyond managing any single optimization category in isolation.

‍

In fact, it’s become clear that we're about something much bigger than management, or efficiency, or even optimization. We're about clarity. The kind of clarity that can only come from intelligence that considers the whole picture, reaches deep into the data, and surfaces the connections and patterns that make all the difference.

What Lucend means
‍

Our new name, Lucend, draws inspiration from lucere, the Latin word that means “to shine, to bring light.”
‍

In our case, this is as much a promise as it is a name.
‍

Every day, in data centers around the world, millions of signals flow through thousands of sensors. Temperature shifts. Power draws. Humidity changes. Fan speeds. The patterns are there and waiting, but they're hidden in plain sight, invisible to even the most experienced operators.

‍

‍

‍

‍

Our Transparent AI cuts through the noise, revealing the connections, events, and patterns that matter most in your data center. But unlike black-box systems that ask for blind faith, Lucend shows its work at every step with transparent data trails, verifiable projections, and clear implementation paths.
‍

We don't replace the people who run data centers. Instead, we empower them, giving them exactly the operational intelligence they need to transform uncertainty and “firefighting” into confident, proactive action. This is the power of machine intelligence designed for human excellence. This is the power of Lucend.

‍We bring your entire operation into the light.


What’s staying the same
‍

Everything that matters remains unchanged: The platform you trust, the team behind it, and the principles that guide us. These are the guiding lights that have helped customers across dozens of global facilities achieve dramatic efficiency gains and they’ll remain the cornerstone of our project.

  • Human in control. Lucend is about empowering the people who run data centers. Our platform delivers insights and recommendations, but humans remain firmly in control of every action.
  • Transparent AI. Unlike black-box systems, Lucend makes its reasoning visible and verifiable, with clear data trails for every recommendation.
  • Operational trust. Measurable savings and sustained gains without risk or compromise. We deliver real, quantifiable results without risking uptime, without new CapEx, and without disrupting existing operations.

‍

‍

‍

We're excited for what's ahead. And we're grateful you're part of this evolution. Here's to bringing clarity to complexity—today, tomorrow, and beyond.

‍

Jasper de Vries & René Gompel
Co-Founders, Lucend
